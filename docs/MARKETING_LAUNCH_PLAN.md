# Squares CORE Framework Launch: Marketing Plan

> **Created:** January 2025
> **Status:** Ready for Execution
> **Mission:** Help people understand each other's political stances better and reduce extremist views of opposing positions

---

## Executive Summary

Squares is transitioning from the TAMER framework (5 dimensions) to CORE (4 dimensions) - a more intuitive, memorable political values assessment. The CORE framework's elegance (one letter per dimension forming a 4-letter "call sign") creates significant viral potential.

**Key Strategic Insight:** Position CORE as a bridge-building tool, not another divisive political quiz. The goal is understanding, not judgment.

## 6. Reducing Polarization Messaging

### Core Narrative

"CORE doesn't tell you who's right or wrong. It shows you why people disagreeâ€”and where they might find common ground."

### Key Messages

1. **"Disagreement is dimensional."**
   You might disagree on economics but agree on civil liberties. Traditional labels hide this.

2. **"Understanding before judgment."**
   When you know someone is ANMT, you understand their priors. You can disagree with conclusions while understanding values.

3. **"16 types, infinite conversations."**
   Every call sign represents a coherent worldview. None are inherently good or bad.

4. **"Your opposite isn't your enemy."**
   Find someone with the opposite CORE. Talk to them. You'll disagree on methods but might share goals.

### Cross-Spectrum Messaging

| Leaning | Message |
|---------|---------|
| Liberty | "CORE doesn't judge. Whether you value individual freedom or collective security, your position is valid." |
| Authority | "Social order matters. CORE recognizes that some people prioritize stability and institutions." |
| Global | "International cooperation has solved problems no nation could alone." |
| National | "National identity and sovereignty matter. CORE doesn't pathologize prioritizing your community." |
| Market | "Markets have lifted billions from poverty. CORE shows how your economic philosophy connects to your broader worldview." |
| Social | "Collective action solves collective problems. CORE recognizes redistribution as an expression of values about fairness." |

### Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Cross-bloc reply rate | >30% of threads | Shows dialogue happening |
| "Opposite" feature usage | 25% of completers | Curiosity about other perspectives |
| Negative sentiment | <10% | People feel seen, not judged |
| Return visits after dialogue | +50% vs. solo | Engagement drives retention |
