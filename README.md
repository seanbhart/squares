# Squares.vote

An open-source framework for mapping political positions across key policy dimensions.

## Square Typology

The Squares typology uses a seven-point spectrum for each issue, allowing individuals to identify their position on multiple policy dimensions. Each color represents a distinct stance, creating a multi-dimensional political profile.

### Policy Dimensions

The **TAME-R** framework measures positions across five core policy areas:

- **T**rade
- **A**bortion  
- **M**igration / Immigration
- **E**conomics
- **R**ights

### Verbose Examples

**Trade Policy**
* 游릵 **Free trade** - Eliminate tariffs, join multilateral agreements, minimal barriers
* 游릱 **Minimal tariffs** - Low tariffs for most goods, focus on trade expansion
* 游릴 **Selective trade agreements** - Free trade with allies, targeted tariffs on competitors
* 游릳 **Balanced tariffs** - Moderate tariffs to protect some domestic industries
* 游릲 **Strategic protections** - Higher tariffs on key industries (steel, agriculture, tech)
* 游린 **Heavy tariffs** - Broad tariffs to prioritize domestic production
* 拘勇 **Closed economy** - Import restrictions, self-sufficiency focus, limited trade

**Abortion**
* 游릵 **Partial birth abortion** - Legalize partial birth abortion
* 游릱 **Limit after viability** - Legalize abortion after viability, but before birth
* 游릴 **Limit after third trimester** - Legalize abortion after third trimester, but before birth
* 游릳 **Limit after second trimester** - Legalize abortion after second trimester, but before birth
* 游릲 **Limit after first trimester** - Legalize abortion after first trimester, but before birth
* 游린 **Limit after heartbeat detection** - Legalize abortion after heartbeat detection, but before birth
* 拘勇 **No exceptions allowed** - No abortion unless to save mother's life

**Migration / Immigration**
* 游릵 **Open borders** - No restrictions, visa-free travel, easy pathways to citizenship
* 游릱 **Easy pathways to citizenship** - Visa requirements, naturalization process, easy citizenship
* 游릴 **Expanded quotas** - Higher immigration limits, family reunification, skilled migration
* 游릳 **Current restrictions** - Moderate immigration, selective visas, family reunification
* 游릲 **Reduced quotas** - Lower immigration limits, selective visas, family reunification
* 游린 **Strict limits only** - Limited immigration, selective visas, family reunification
* 拘勇 **No immigration** - No immigration, no visas, no citizenship

**Economics**
* 游릵 **Pure free market** - Minimal government, privatize most services, no safety net
* 游릱 **Minimal regulation** - Low taxes, light regulation, basic safety net only
* 游릴 **Market-based with safety net** - Private economy with unemployment, healthcare subsidies
* 游릳 **Balanced public-private** - Mixed economy with public utilities and private markets
* 游릲 **Strong social programs** - Universal healthcare, education, significant welfare state
* 游린 **Extensive public ownership** - Government owns key industries, strong redistribution
* 拘勇 **Full state control** - Central planning, most property/industry state-owned

**Rights (Civil Liberties)**
* 游릵 **Full legal equality** - Marriage equality, adoption rights, anti-discrimination laws in all sectors
* 游릱 **Protections with few limits** - Legal recognition and protections, some religious exemptions
* 游릴 **Protections with some limits** - Anti-discrimination in public sector, religious freedom in private sector
* 游릳 **Tolerance without endorsement** - Legal to exist, no special protections or recognition
* 游릲 **Traditional definitions only** - Marriage/family defined traditionally, no legal accommodations
* 游린 **No legal recognition** - No legal status for same-sex relationships or gender identity changes
* 拘勇 **Criminalization** - Laws prohibiting LGBTQ+ expression or relationships
